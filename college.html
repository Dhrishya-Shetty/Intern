About Us

Learn about our institution's history, mission, and commitment to excellence.

Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology & Management is situated in Udupi in Karnataka state of India. Established in 2010, SMVITM is a Private college. The college is accredited by AICTE. Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology & Management offers 6 courses across 1 streams namely Engineering..Popular degrees offered at Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology & Management include B.Tech.Besides a robust teaching pedagogy, Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology & Management is also a leader in research and innovation.Focus is given to activities beyond academics at Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology & Management, which is evident from its infrastructure, extracurricular activities and national & international collaborations. The placement at Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology & Management is varied, with recruitment options both in corporates and public sector as well as entrepreneurship.
